Money by SanD

Make budgeting simple, with this beautifully designed, simple app for tracking and understanding your everyday money flows.

Home Page

The Home Page lets you see and edit all your transactions sorted according to date.

Accounts Page

As you start using Money, you will create accounts as your first step. Accounts is where your money is stored, an example could be your Bank account, or your wallet, or even your safe. Here you will see where you money is stored and how much there is. This page also shows the money you have lended to others or the money you have loaned from others.

Categories Page

Categories are the places where you spend your money. This could be Food and Drinks, Rent, Fuel, Electricty, Shopping and anything else you can think of. Categories are divided into 2 sections, the incomes and expenses. Incomes are the places where get your money, like your Salary, or Allowance or the money from your Businesses.

Analytics Page

When you want to know your net worth, net liabilities or your spending patterns, this is the place to come.
